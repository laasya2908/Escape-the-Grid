ABOUT THE GAME:

  • A maze is a type of puzzle games where a player moves in complex and branched passages to find a particular target or location. It is a path or collection of paths, typically from an entrance to a goal.
  • Mazes can help children think outside the box, finding unconventional methods to solve a maze much more easily, that could stick in their minds for other situations outside of play.

  • If the player is unable to find the path within the given time then they lose the game on that Map.
  • Finally the path is displayed using a path finding algorithm.
